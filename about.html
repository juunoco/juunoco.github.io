About Us

We're a team of two in New Zealand, ready to tackle new products and designs. We've dabbled in anything ranging from vanilla JS to complex AR projects on Hololens, we always try to innovate across different mediums and are eager to keep you impressed


Donna Dychinco Developer - Artist - Project Manager

Donna has left Adobe Illustrator running on her PC since 2015, she has such a sharp critique that she will make the same design over and over again with minute changes. A lot of our front-end designs are decided by her and she makes sure our projects are progressing within deadlines.

Jun Kagaya Developer - Sound Design - Technical Manager

When Jun can be found away from the imaginary world, he is usually found creating a new idea. He specializes in sound design and the creation of immersive environments. A lot of the time, he chooses the technical tools to use on a project.
